Dumpster Bear

Decades of using garbage as a primary food source has obliterated the Dumpster Bear's olfactory senses. This leaves it immune to it's own body odour which in turn acts a natural defense mechanism

  • Scientific Name: Dougthathuglus
  • Average Life Span: 32 years
  • Food Source: Brioche & Nutella
  • Habitat: Berlin Dumpsters

The Dumpster Bear is a formidable creature that lives a secluded life in surburban Berlin. Is trained in the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu but prefers to disarm its pray with bad jokes and terrible puns.
